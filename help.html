üöÄ Getting Started

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
  2. Fill in your basic information (name, email, phone, city)
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile with a profile picture

Once registered, you can start posting items and browsing listings immediately!

You can buy and sell a wide range of pre-loved items:

  • Electronics: Laptops, phones, gaming consoles, cameras
  • Furniture: Chairs, tables, beds, storage units
  • Appliances: Microwaves, refrigerators, washing machines
  • Books: Textbooks, novels, study materials
  • Clothing: Branded clothes, shoes, accessories
  • Sports Equipment: Gym equipment, sports gear

Prohibited items: Illegal items, weapons, drugs, counterfeit goods, adult content, perishable food.

WhatsApp integration makes communication seamless:

  • When you click "WhatsApp" on any listing, it opens WhatsApp with a pre-written message
  • The message includes item details to start the conversation
  • You can then chat directly with the buyer/seller
  • Arrange meetings, negotiate prices, and share additional photos

Note: Make sure your phone number is correctly entered in your profile for smooth WhatsApp communication.

üì¶ Posting Items

Posting an item takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Click "Post Item" from the homepage or your profile
  2. Add a clear, descriptive title (minimum 5 characters)
  3. Write a detailed description (minimum 20 characters)
  4. Select the appropriate category and condition
  5. Set your asking price (you can mark it as negotiable)
  6. Upload up to 4 high-quality photos
  7. Choose your city and area
  8. Review your listing and click "Post Item"

Your item will be live immediately and visible to potential buyers!

Great listings have these elements:

  • Clear Photos: Well-lit, multiple angles, show any wear/damage
  • Honest Description: Mention age, condition, reason for selling
  • Fair Pricing: Research similar items, be realistic
  • Complete Information: Include brand, model, specifications
  • Quick Response: Reply to inquiries promptly

Pro Tips: Mention if you have original box/receipt, highlight unique features, be transparent about any issues.

Yes! You can edit your listings anytime:

  1. Go to "My Items" from your profile menu
  2. Find the item you want to edit
  3. Click "Edit" button
  4. Update title, description, price, or photos
  5. Save your changes

You can also mark items as "Sold" or delete listings you no longer want to display.

We compress images to ensure fast loading and better user experience:

  • Images are automatically optimized for web viewing
  • Total upload size is limited to 100KB for all photos combined
  • This ensures quick loading on all devices and internet connections
  • Quality is maintained while reducing file size

Tips: Upload high-resolution photos - our system will optimize them. Avoid uploading already compressed or low-quality images.

üí∞ Buying & Selling

Contacting sellers is easy with WhatsApp integration:

  1. Find an item you're interested in
  2. Click "WhatsApp" button on the listing
  3. This opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message about the item
  4. Send the message to start the conversation
  5. Ask questions, negotiate price, arrange meeting

Tips: Be specific about what you want to know, be polite, and respond promptly to build trust.

MoveOut Market doesn't handle payments directly. Buyers and sellers arrange payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Most common and safest for local transactions
  • UPI/Digital Wallets: Quick transfers through PayTM, PhonePe, GooglePay
  • Bank Transfer: For higher value items, after inspection
  • Cash: For in-person meetings and immediate exchanges

Safety Tip: Never make full payment without seeing/inspecting the item first.

Choose safe, public locations for meetups:

  • Recommended: Malls, coffee shops, metro stations, busy market areas
  • For furniture: Apartment complexes, gated communities (with permission)
  • For electronics: Places where you can test the item (cafes with power outlets)
  • Avoid: Isolated areas, late night meetings, private residences (unless you're comfortable)

Pro Tip: Bring a friend if you're unsure, meet during daytime, inform someone about your meeting.

üõ°Ô∏è Safety & Security

Look for these signs of genuine users:

  • Complete Profile: Real name, photo, verified phone number
  • Clear Communication: Proper language, specific questions about the item
  • Reasonable Requests: Wants to see item before buying, asks relevant questions
  • Flexible Meeting: Suggests safe, public meeting places

Red Flags: Overly eager to buy without questions, offers way above asking price, wants to send courier, asks for advance payment, pushy behavior.

If you suspect fraudulent activity:

  1. Don't proceed: Trust your instincts and stop communication
  2. Document everything: Take screenshots of conversations
  3. Report the user: Use our reporting system or contact support
  4. Block the user: Prevent further contact
  5. Warn others: Share experience (without personal details) to help community

Contact Support: Reach out to us at support@moveoutmarket.com or WhatsApp +81-0987654321

Keep your information safe:

  • Share minimal details: Only provide what's necessary for the transaction
  • Use WhatsApp: Keep communication within the platform initially
  • Don't share: Full address, financial details, personal ID numbers
  • Meet in public: Avoid giving away your home address
  • Be cautious with photos: Avoid showing personal items/documents in item photos

üë§ Account & Profile

To update your profile:

  1. Click on your name in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click "Edit Profile" button
  4. Update your information (name, phone, location, bio)
  5. Save changes

You can also change your profile avatar, update notification settings, and manage your account preferences from the profile page.

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Go to your Profile page
  2. Scroll down to "Account Management" section
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm by typing "DELETE" as prompted
  5. Your account and all data will be permanently removed

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your listings, messages, and account data will be permanently deleted.

üîß Troubleshooting

Common reasons why posting might fail:

  • Not logged in: Make sure you're signed in to your account
  • Incomplete form: Fill all required fields (title, description, price, category)
  • Title too short: Title must be at least 5 characters
  • Description too short: Description must be at least 20 characters
  • Images too large: Total image size must be under 100KB
  • Invalid phone number: Enter a valid mobile number

If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or contact support.

If the WhatsApp button doesn't work:

  • Check if WhatsApp is installed: Make sure you have WhatsApp on your phone
  • Allow pop-ups: Enable pop-ups for our website in your browser
  • Try different browser: Some browsers block WhatsApp links
  • Copy phone number: If button fails, copy the phone number and message manually
  • Use WhatsApp Web: Try opening WhatsApp Web in another tab first

If images won't upload, try these solutions:

  • Check file size: Each image should be under 25KB after compression
  • Use supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF are supported
  • Limit number: Upload maximum 4 images at once
  • Check internet connection: Ensure stable internet connection
  • Try smaller images: Resize images before uploading
  • Clear browser cache: Refresh the page and try again

Our system automatically compresses images to under 100KB total for optimal performance.
